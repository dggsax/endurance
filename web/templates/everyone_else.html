To Everyone Else

Letter from the class

To everyone else,

The MIT community is joined in infinitely many ways and through infinitely many means. There are many more members of the community than just the students robbed of their ceremonies.

We would love to hear stories from everyone - current students, members of the Emma Rogers Society, facilities and staff members, alumni of different years, Boston and Cambridge community members, MIT Corporation members, campus police officers, members of administration, presidents of MIT, and absolutely everybody else - of how life has changed in these strange times.

We look forward to hearing perspectives on the crisis, on life, and on this period of adjustment from each and every person who would like to contribute their story.

Thank you for helping to hold all of us together.
